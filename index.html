Background

Warhammer 40k is set 39,000 years in the future. Humanity has expanded across the stars and conquered most of the Milky Way Galaxy. To do so they have created a new type of super soldier, Space Marines, that are genetically modified to fight wars that normal humans can't. Humanity comes close to finishing their great conquest until their greatest general, Horus Lupercal turns on the Imperium of Man and wages the bloodiest civil war imaginable.

Based off of a table top game designed in the 80's, Warhammer 40k has exploded in popularity and can be found in all parts of the world. It includes miniatures, books, videogames and movies. The game features several prominent races that an enthusiast can choose to build an army from. The most popular races include Space Marines, Imperial Guard, Eldar, Orks, Chaos Forces, and Tyranids. Models and Codex (Books with Army specific information) can be purchased and painted to be able to play the table top version of the game. Games Workshop is the original founding company that designs and sells the miniatures, however they can be purchased from local hoby shops as well.

In addition to the table top game, and because of it's metoric rise to popularity. Games Workshop also released many books that are available on Black Library. These books dive deeper into the Lore of prominent characters found in the table top game, as well as provide a rich history of the events leading up to the century the table top game is set in. The books can be purchased in both written and audio form and are also available on Audible.
